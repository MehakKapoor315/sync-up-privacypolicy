We may collect basic app usage information such as screens visited, selected companies, and app interactions to improve the user experience, fix bugs, and understand how the app is being used.

We may collect general device information such as device model, operating system version, and app version for debugging, performance monitoring, and improving app reliability.

SyncUp uses Firebase services, including Firebase Firestore, to store and retrieve company holiday data. Firebase may process limited technical data required to provide database and app functionality.
